Ayurveda is an ancient system of medicine that has been practiced in India for thousands of years. It is based on the belief that all aspects of life and health are interconnected and need to be in harmony for an individual to stay healthy and peaceful. Thanks to a revival of the science of Ayurveda in the recent years, it is now easier than ever to incorporate Ayurvedic lifestyle practices in our everyday lives.

<Blockquote name="Vd. Ankita Singhal">
 You don't need to be health expert to incorporate Ayurvedic lifestyle into your life. Start with small changes and see a better you!
</Blockquote>

Ayurveda suggests that everyone is unique and has a unique energy makeup, and what works for one person may not work for another. Therefore, it is important to be mindful of your body’s needs and find the best options for improving your health and wellbeing. Here are some of the basic principles of Ayurvedic lifestyle:

• Eating an organic, whole food diet.

• Drinking Ayurvedic teas and herbs to balance your doshas.

• Choosing a daily routine that works for you.

• Practicing meditative or yoga exercises to relax and nurture your body.

• Connecting with nature and spending time in natural sunlight for reducing stress.

• Considering the use of essential oils, aromatherapy and Ayurvedic massage for relaxation and rejuvenation.

• Believing and honoring your body’s natural cycles.

• Getting enough sleep to give your body and mind enough rest to stay healthy.

By following these simple steps, you can start to bring balance and harmony to your daily life. Ayurvedic practices are often gentle and effective and offer solutions for many lifestyle-related health problems.
